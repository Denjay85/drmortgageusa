Loan Options You Can Qualify For

You get matched with the best loan options across many lenders. I compare everything for you so you understand your numbers before you make a move.

FHA Loans

Minimum 3.5 percent down. Strong option for buyers building or rebuilding credit.

VA Loans

Zero down. No PMI. The strongest financing available for eligible veterans and active duty.

Conventional Loans

Starting at 3 percent down. Best rates and lowest costs for strong credit.

USDA Loans

Zero down in qualifying rural areas. Lower monthly cost for buyers outside metro zones.

Bank Statement Loans

Twelve to twenty four months of business deposits used as income. Ideal for self employed buyers who do not show high taxable income.

DSCR Investor Loans

Approval based on rental income, not personal income. Clean solution for investors expanding their portfolio.

I served five years on active duty with two combat deployments and continue to serve in the Navy Reserve, totaling fifteen years of military service. I moved into the mortgage industry in 2020 with the same mindset the military and social work taught me. Stay disciplined, stay accurate, and keep people calm when the pressure is real.
