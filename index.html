About Dr. MortgageUSA

I'm Dennis Ross, but you can call me Dr. Mortgage USA. I'm a Navy veteran with fifteen years of service, five years on active duty with two combat deployments, and ten years in the reserves. I bring that background into every part of my work as a Florida mortgage broker based in Orlando.

What sets me apart:

Veterans come first.

I lived this life. I know how VA loans actually work. I fight to get funding fees waived, help you secure property tax exemptions, and close clean VA loans without stress.

I work with 79 different lenders.

As an independent broker, I shop the market for you. I compare real time rates from 79 lenders to get you the strongest financing. If you need down payment assistance or have a unique income profile, I find the program that fits you.

You will understand every number.

I walk you through your figures on Zoom so you see everything in real time. I send plain language follow up emails. You get clear answers within 24 hours. No jargon. No confusion.

My Mission: I bring the same integrity and determination from my Navy career into my work today. I help make homeownership simple, affordable, and low stress for every family I serve in Florida or every family relocating to our beautiful state of Florida.
