How it works

Simple process, clear numbers, no surprises.

You get a guided path from first question to clear to close. I stay plugged in with you and your agent at every step.

Step 1

60 second qualifier and call

You fill out the short form. I review your answers, then we connect on a call or Zoom. I learn your story, your income, and your comfort zone for a payment.

Step 2

Numbers, options, and a written plan

I check credit the right way, run your file through my 79 lender options, and build side by side numbers for VA, FHA, and conventional where you qualify.

Loan programs

VA, FHA, and first time buyer loans in Florida.

I am a broker in Orlando who serves every family in Florida. We pick the right program based on your goals, not a quota.

VA loans in Florida

You earned this benefit. I treat it that way. I help active duty, Guard, and veterans use VA to own in Florida.

  • Zero down options for eligible buyers
  • No monthly mortgage insurance
  • Strategies for VA disability and property tax savings
  • Guidance on multi unit, assumptions, and future refi plans

FHA and first time buyer programs

FHA is flexible when life has had some bumps. I use it to help first time buyers, credit rebuilders, and folks with higher debt.

  • Low down payment options
  • Lenient credit and debt guidelines
  • Florida first time buyer assistance where available
  • Clear roadmap to refinance when your profile improves

Conventional and move up borrowers

When your credit and income are strong, conventional can work best. I show you where it wins and where it does not.

  • Primary homes, second homes, and investment options
  • Side by side comparisons to VA or FHA when you qualify
  • Strategies for keeping or selling your current home
  • Long term plan for future refinances and equity

About Dennis

Why I became Dr. Mortgage USA.
